</h2>

<div class="cn-block" markdown="1">

赵官豪，中国科学技术大学人工智能与数据科学学院博士生，隶属于认知智能全国重点实验室，导师为[陈恩红](http://staff.ustc.edu.cn/~cheneh/)教授和[黄振亚](http://staff.ustc.edu.cn/~huangzhy/)副教授。主要研究方向为强化学习、模仿学习与扩散模型，及其在智能体能力建模与评估、多智能体协作中的应用。

</div>
<div class="en-block" markdown="1">

Guanhao Zhao is a Ph.D. student at the School of Artificial Intelligence and Data Science, University of Science and Technology of China (USTC), affiliated with the State Key Laboratory of Cognitive Intelligence. He is supervised by [Prof. Enhong Chen](http://staff.ustc.edu.cn/~cheneh/) and [Assoc. Prof. Zhenya Huang](http://staff.ustc.edu.cn/~huangzhy/). His research interests lie in reinforcement learning, imitation learning, and diffusion models, with applications to agent capability modeling, evaluation, and multi-agent collaboration.

</div>
